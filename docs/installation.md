# Installation

Welcome to CampaignBay! This guide will walk you through installing both the free CampaignBay plugin and the optional CampaignBayPro extension.

## Requirements

Before you begin, please ensure your WordPress environment meets the following minimum requirements:

- **WordPress:** Version 5.8 or higher
- **PHP:** Version 7.0 or higher
- **WooCommerce:** The WooCommerce plugin must be installed and activated.

::: tip
If you are unsure about any of these requirements, please contact your hosting provider for assistance.
:::

## Installing CampaignBay (Free)

The free version of CampaignBay is available on WordPress.org and can be installed directly from your dashboard.

### Option 1: Install from WordPress Dashboard (Recommended)

1. Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Plugins → Add New**.
3. In the search field, type **"CampaignBay"** and press Enter.
4. Find CampaignBay in the search results and click **Install Now**.
5. After installation completes, click **Activate**.

### Option 2: Manual Upload via .zip File

If you have the plugin's `.zip` file:

1. Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Plugins → Add New**.
3. Click the **Upload Plugin** button at the top of the screen.
4. Click **Choose File** and select the `campaignbay.zip` file from your computer.
5. Click **Install Now**.
6. Once complete, click **Activate Plugin**.

### Verify the Installation

After activation, you should see a "Plugin activated" confirmation message. Look for the new **"CampaignBay"** menu item in your WordPress admin sidebar.

## Installing CampaignBayPro

CampaignBayPro is a premium extension that adds advanced features to the free plugin. [**Purchase CampaignBayPro**](https://wpanchorbay.com/campaignbay) to unlock BOGO Advanced, Product In Cart discounts, and Advanced Conditions.

::: warning Important
You must install and activate the free CampaignBay plugin **before** installing CampaignBayPro.
:::

### Step 1: Install the Free Plugin First

If you haven't already, follow the instructions above to install and activate the free CampaignBay plugin.

### Step 2: Download CampaignBayPro

Download the `campaignbaypro.zip` file from:

- Your purchase confirmation email, or
- Your account page at [wpanchorbay.com](https://wpanchorbay.com)

### Step 3: Upload and Activate

1. Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Plugins → Add New**.
3. Click the **Upload Plugin** button.
4. Click **Choose File** and select the `campaignbaypro.zip` file.
5. Click **Install Now**.
6. Once complete, click **Activate Plugin**.

### Step 4: Activate Your License

After activating the plugin, you need to activate your license key to unlock Pro features and receive updates.

1. Navigate to **CampaignBay → Settings**.
2. Click the **Advanced** tab.
3. In the **License Key** field, enter the license key from your purchase confirmation.
4. Click the **Activate** button.

::: tip
Your license key was sent to your email address when you purchased CampaignBayPro. If you can't find it, check your spam folder or visit your account page at [wpanchorbay.com](https://wpanchorbay.com).
:::

Once activated, you'll see a "License Active" confirmation, and all Pro features will be unlocked.

## Troubleshooting

### WooCommerce is not active

CampaignBay is an add-on for WooCommerce and cannot run without it. Please ensure WooCommerce is installed and activated before activating CampaignBay.

### CampaignBay is not active (Pro only)

CampaignBayPro requires the free CampaignBay plugin (version 1.1.0 or higher) to be installed and active. If you see a warning message, please update or activate the free plugin first.

### License activation failed (Pro only)

If license activation fails:

- Double-check that you've entered the license key correctly (no extra spaces).
- Ensure your site has an active internet connection.
- Verify that your license hasn't exceeded the allowed number of site activations.
- Contact support at [wpanchorbay.com/support/](https://wpanchorbay.com/support/) for assistance.
